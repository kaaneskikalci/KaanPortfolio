For my university Blender class, I wanted to push my skills further so I designed all of the environment and objects in this scene myself. For the
character I used a ready-made astronaut asset, since my own sculpting wasn't
where I wanted it to be yet. I rigged the astronaut and created all of the
materials and lighting for the scene and every object.
