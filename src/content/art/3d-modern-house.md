For my university Blender class, I wanted to push my skills further, so I rebuilt
the modern house from a Udemy course I had taken — this time entirely on my own,
from scratch. I modeled the exterior and interior, set up the materials, and
staged each room.

The most challenging part was the lighting: getting the night-time mood right,
balancing the interior lamps against the exterior and pool lighting so every
render felt natural rather than flat.
